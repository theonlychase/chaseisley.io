Shopagram
by Chase Isley
Project Description
Many e-commerce businesses feel that Instagram doesn't provide a ton of value because you can't put links on your posts or in the descriptions. This prevents customers from going directly to the website from a post. So if a customer sees a posts they like, they would have to leave Instagram, open a browser, search for the website, then search for the product. Depending on how user-friendly the site is can cause problems. Shopagram eliminates these problems.

Customers can create an account, connect to Instagram and set up their shop within minutes. When the shop is complete, you paste your link in your Instagram profile. When a customer sees a product they like, they would click on your link which would take them to your shop page where they can easily find the product they want and checkout.
