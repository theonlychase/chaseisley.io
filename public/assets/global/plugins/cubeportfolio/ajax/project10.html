The iHelper
by Chase Isley & Alexander Scheuch
Project Description
Throughout college, Alex and I worked in tech support for Apple. We were asked by friends/family constantly how to use/fix their Apple products. All of the questions inspired us to write the iHelper.

We were recent business grads at the time and thought we could make some money off of the idea. We really didn't have any experience in Photoshop or selling products online, much less building websites.

We would meet up 1-2 times a week at The Beat Coffeehouse. We taught ourselves how to use Photoshop. We learned wordpress and surprisingly built a really nice looking site.

This project is actually what started my career in web development. To download a copy of the book, click the link to the right.
