Bonavista Optics Business App
by Chase Isley
Project Description
This app was built in Ionic for Bonavista Optics so that clients can easily schedule appointments and request refills for their contact lenses.

- Send Emails directly from the app without leaving the app.
- Schedule appointments
- Beautiful, User-Friendly layout
- Features a Gallery of Products
- Geolocation with Google Maps
- Can Rate the App in the Android/iOS store from within the app.
- and more!

- To view the app, download the Ionic View app from the iOS or Android market.
- Once downloaded, tap Preview an app and enter in the app code 36058885.
